Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At ZawajPlus, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in accordance with Islamic principles of trust and confidentiality. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Islamic marriage platform.

1. Types of Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: This includes but is not limited to your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, location, and other contact details provided during account registration or usage of our services.
  • Profile Information: Religious preferences, marriage intentions, family details, education, occupation, and other matrimonial preferences.
  • Media Content: Photos and videos you upload to your profile, which are stored securely and shared only with your explicit consent.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with our services, including but not limited to IP addresses, device information, and browsing history.
  • Communication Data: Messages, video call logs, and other communications within the platform for safety and quality assurance.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and gather information about your preferences.

2. Use of Your Personal Data

We may use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain our services, including personalized content and features, profile matching, and facilitating connections.
  • Communication: To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, send important notifications, and provide customer support.
  • Safety and Security: To verify your identity, prevent fraud, ensure platform safety, and maintain the integrity of our Islamic community.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns, improve our services, and customize content based on user preferences while maintaining anonymity.
  • Islamic Compliance: To ensure all interactions and connections align with Islamic principles and community standards.

3. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We may disclose your personal data in the following situations:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your information with other users or third parties.
  • Family Involvement: With Walis (guardians) and family members as required by Islamic marriage practices, with your knowledge and consent.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests or court orders.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Business Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction.
  • Safety and Protection: To protect the safety, rights, and property of our users and the broader community.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Control who can see your profile and media
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Object to certain data processing activities

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, and maintain platform safety and security.
